About us

Micropoint Computers Pvt. Ltd. (MPCL)

Micropoint Computers Pvt. Ltd. (MPCL) is one of India’s leading IT Infrastructure Solutions and Services Company. We are an ISO 9001:2015 certified with more than 3 decades of presence. We are a team of 700+ skilled personnel. The management team carries a combined average technical experience of 20+ years.

WHAT WE DO ?

MPCL offers solutions for customers across many verticals. We combine state of the art IT products, project roll-out skills and our infrastructure management services and thereby cover entire lifecycle of your IT setup.

OUR OFFERINGS

We provide comprehensive IT infrastructure solutions for data centers, covering Servers, Storage, Converged and Hyper-Converged infrastructure, Backup, Data Protection, Network & Security, and OS & middleware. Our services include end user computing solutions and data center build services for green-field projects. The "Data Science Platform" accelerates AI and ML projects. MPCL has deployed 50,000+ end user devices and 5,000 servers nationwide, with service contracts for data center assets. Our distribution division offers accessories from leading OEMs like HPI, Dell, Lenovo, and Acer.

OUR APPROACH

Our objective / mission is to build a loyal customer base. For this we use consultative and collaborative approach to design fitting solution and optimal BOM for customers. Our services cover Deployment, Project roll-out and Infrastructure management services. We carry some of the special skills and have demonstrated them well. These cover / include design and execution of HPC Clusters, Infrastructure for SAP HANA. Thus we support business critical environments and the research oriented institutes equally well. In a way we cover entire life-cycle of customer’s IT Infrastructure and support a sustainable IT platform.
